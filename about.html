About Me

Hey there!
I’m Termwan Chiangpradit, but you can call me Snow! And no, it’s not because I’m obsessed with snow (though, I do love it). So why Snow? Well, here’s a fun little story: I’m from Thailand, where everyone has a nickname picked by their parents from birth. My mom was totally into ice cream when she was pregnant – even in the cold or snow! She almost named me “Ice Cream,” but then she thought “Snow White” sounded pretty and magical. So, she went with Snow White... and I just go by Snow for short!

Now for the rest of the scoop: I was born in Seattle, WA, but grew up in Bangkok, Thailand – so Thai is actually my first language! I came back to the States when I was 13, and at 24, I became a flight attendant and lived in Dubai for almost 5 years! It was an incredible experience—I traveled all over, tried tons of amazing food, and met some truly fascinating people.
